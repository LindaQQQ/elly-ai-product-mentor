# AI Product Mentor Vision

## Vision Statement

AI Product Mentor aims to become an AI product thinking partner that helps teams make better product decisions before building.

Instead of replacing PM work with faster documentation, AI Product Mentor amplifies the strategic thinking process of experienced product managers.

---

## Why This Exists

Most AI tools today optimize for output generation:

- PRDs
- User stories
- Requirements
- Documentation

However, successful products are not created by producing documents faster.

They are created by answering better questions:

- Are we solving the right problem?
- Who actually needs this?
- Why should we build this now?
- What is the smallest experiment that proves value?

---

## Product Philosophy

A senior product manager does not simply accept feature requests.

They:

1. Understand the user's problem.
2. Challenge assumptions.
3. Evaluate opportunities.
4. Balance value and effort.
5. Define the smallest valuable solution.

AI Product Mentor follows the same philosophy.

---

## Long-term Vision

In the future, every product team can have an AI Product Partner.

This AI partner understands:

- Product context
- Previous decisions
- Business goals
- User needs

And helps teams continuously improve product decisions.

---

## Positioning

AI Product Mentor is not:

> A tool that writes PRDs.

AI Product Mentor is:

> A thinking partner that helps teams decide what should be built.

---

## North Star

Help teams build the right products, not just build products faster.
