# AI Planning Prototype Evaluation

## Goal

Validate whether the AI Planning workflow helps PMs reason about an AI feature
before investing in a production Planning Agent or backend API.

## Core Questions

1. Can a PM complete the input form using a real product idea without help?
2. Does the AI Readiness result feel understandable and credible?
3. Is the Workflow/RAG/Agent/Memory/Tools recommendation actionable?
4. Does the result reveal a risk or unknown the PM had not considered?
5. Do the next questions help the PM move the plan forward?
6. Would the PM use the workflow again with another real requirement?

## Prototype Success Signals

- At least 80% of test users complete the form without facilitator help.
- At least 70% can explain why the suggested architecture was recommended.
- At least 70% identify one useful new risk or question.
- At least 60% say the result would change or sharpen their next planning step.
- Qualitative feedback does not reveal a missing critical input category.

These are discovery thresholds, not production quality targets.

## Suggested Research Session

1. Ask the participant to bring a real AI feature idea.
2. Observe completion without explaining the fields.
3. Ask the participant to think aloud while reading the result.
4. Record confusing language, missing context, and disputed recommendations.
5. Ask what they would do next and whether the result changed that action.

## Production Evaluation Later

Once the result is generated by an AI Planning Agent, add evaluation for:

- AI suitability classification accuracy
- Architecture recommendation quality
- Risk coverage
- Unsupported claims and hallucinations
- Consistency across equivalent inputs
- Latency and cost
- Safety and sensitive-data handling
