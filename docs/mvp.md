# AI Product Mentor MVP Definition

## MVP Goal

The goal of AI Product Mentor MVP is to validate one core hypothesis:

> Can AI help product teams make better decisions before development begins?

The MVP focuses on product evaluation, not document generation.

---

# Target Users

## Primary User

Product Managers

Especially PMs who need to:

- Evaluate new product ideas
- Prioritize features
- Align with stakeholders
- Reduce development waste

---

## Secondary Users

- Product leaders
- Startup founders
- Engineering leads
- Business teams proposing new initiatives

---

# Core User Problem

Product teams often receive ideas from:

- Users
- Executives
- Business teams
- Market trends

However, teams lack a structured process to evaluate:

- Is this a real problem?
- Who needs it?
- Should we build it now?
- What is the smallest valuable solution?

---

# MVP User Flow
User submits product idea

↓

AI asks clarification questions

↓

AI challenges assumptions

↓

AI evaluates opportunity

↓

AI recommends MVP scope


---

# MVP Capabilities

## 1. Problem Understanding

AI identifies:

- Target user
- User pain point
- Current alternatives
- Problem severity

---

## 2. Product Challenge

AI acts as a senior product mentor.

AI challenges:

- Unvalidated assumptions
- Weak differentiation
- Unclear user value
- Excessive scope

---

## 3. MVP Recommendation

AI recommends:

Build:

- What should be included

Avoid:

- What should not be built initially

Validate:

- What metrics prove value

---

# MVP Output

The user receives:

## Product Assessment

Including:

- Problem clarity score
- Key assumptions
- Risks
- Validation questions
- MVP recommendation

---

# Non Goals

The MVP will not include:

- Full PRD generation
- Project management
- Team collaboration
- Enterprise integrations
- Long-term memory

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if users feel:

"Before building this, I now understand the problem better."

Evaluation metrics:

- Does AI identify important assumptions?
- Does AI provide meaningful challenges?
- Does AI reduce unnecessary scope?
- Would a PM change their decision after using it?
